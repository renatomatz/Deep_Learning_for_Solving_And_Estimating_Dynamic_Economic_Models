# AGENTS.md

## Purpose

This directory contains one curated reading-link guide per lecture, numbered
`lecture_01.md` through `lecture_18.md`.

## Editing

Keep files link-only and keep the lecture numbering zero-padded. Reading bullets
use human-readable citations and short annotations, not BibTeX keys.

If a new source is important enough to add here and is cited by slides or the
companion script, also add or update the corresponding entry in
`../bibliography.bib`.

Do not add PDFs or copied article text here. Link to DOI, arXiv, publisher,
SSRN, author pages, or official online editions.
